Stop Motion for Beginners

Introduction

Stop motion. Very time consuming, needs a lot of dedication, and most of the reward at the end is simply pride in what you have done. If you are looking for anything else such as monetary gain or praise, a fast food job will give you a similar experience with double the reward.

Equipment

You need a nice camera, and a good enough video editing software. I just use my phone camera and the software I use is an iMovie equivalent for windows called Movavi. It is not free and it is not worth it, but it is relatively cheap compared to most video editing programs. When taking pictures, be sure to not touch the camera, as it makes the footage look less stable and shaky.
